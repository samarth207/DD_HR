
# Professional HR & Employee Portal UI Design Prompt

**Role:**
You are a senior UI/UX designer from a world-class product design agency (Linear, Stripe, Notion, Ramp, Rippling, Deel, Jira, Framer quality). Your only task is to design a premium, modern, enterprise-grade HR & Employee Management Portal.

**Important:**
Do NOT build backend logic.
Do NOT generate code.
Focus ONLY on the UI/UX design.

The design should look like a product that costs millions to build—not a generic AI dashboard.

---

# Design Style

Create an elegant enterprise SaaS dashboard.

Style inspiration:

* Linear
* Notion
* Stripe Dashboard
* Rippling
* Deel
* Slack Admin
* Framer
* Vercel Dashboard
* Atlassian
* Monday.com

Avoid:

❌ Bright gradients everywhere

❌ Glassmorphism overload

❌ Huge rounded blobs

❌ Generic AI dashboard look

❌ Neon colors

❌ Cartoon icons

❌ Material Design default layouts

Instead create

* Minimal
* Premium
* Professional
* Clean spacing
* Strong typography
* Soft shadows
* Excellent hierarchy
* Modern cards
* Beautiful charts
* Excellent use of whitespace

The design should immediately feel like an HR product used by companies with 1000+ employees.

# Design Quality

The final result should look like a combination of:

* Rippling
* Deel
* Stripe
* Linear
* Notion
* Framer
* Vercel
* Jira Premium

It should look like a product that a Fortune 500 company would confidently use for HR, attendance, leave management, and sales tracking.

**Important:** Focus entirely on creating a visually exceptional, modern, enterprise-grade interface. Avoid generic AI dashboard patterns, excessive gradients, oversized icons, or template-like layouts. Every screen should feel thoughtfully designed with premium spacing, typography, micro-interactions, and polished components.
